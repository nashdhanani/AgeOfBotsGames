Habib Punja: Strategic 7-Hand War (v5.1.3)

Play: AgeOfBotsGames.com

Habib Punja is a 3-player strategic card game presented as a 7-hand championship. The objective is not just to win every hand, but to win the war by managing risk and avoiding devastating losses.

Core Concepts

The 7-Hand War

  • One hand lost ≠ game lost
  • Avoid catastrophic "nuke" hands
  • Winning 4+ hands typically secures the championship
  • Maintain a "battle vs war" mentality

Builds and Requirements

Requirements increase in complexity across the seven hands.

7Master3 Ladders (fewer buys)⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Buying

Buying adds a card to your hand "out of turn," but comes with a penalty card and reduces your limited buy count.

Publishing

Once you meet the requirement, you can Publish. This locks your cards on the table, safe from point penalties.
Trade-off: Early publishing wins tempo but locks your strategy. Late publishing offers stability but risks being outpaced.

The Glass Vault (Discard Pile)

  • Only the top card is available.
  • When someone takes it, the vault locks until a new discard occurs.

Win Condition

The championship is determined by the lowest score across seven hands. Optimize for:

  • Maximizing hand wins
  • Minimizing "nukes" (large point penalties)
  • Exploiting opponent tendencies (Habot vs Jabot)

The Opponents

Habot (Rabbit) 🐰: Fast, aggressive. Counter by baiting into overcommitting.
Jabot (Tortoise) 🐢: Patient, surgical. Counter by applying tempo pressure.

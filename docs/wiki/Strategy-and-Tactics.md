## Hand-by-Hand Guidance

### Hand 1: Foundations (2 Triples)
- **Focus:** Build two solid triples
- **Risk Level:** Low
- **Tip:** Prioritize pairs, watch for third cards

### Hand 2: Balance (1 Triple + 1 Ladder)
- **Focus:** Commit to one ladder suit early
- **Risk Level:** Medium
- **Tip:** Your triple can come from leftover cards

### Hand 3: Ascent (2 Ladders)
- **Focus:** Choose two complementary suits
- **Risk Level:** Medium-High
- **Tip:** Overlapping suits reduce card competition

### Hand 4: Crowd (3 Triples)
- **Focus:** Accumulate pairs systematically
- **Risk Level:** Medium
- **Tip:** Face cards (J-Q-K) appear more often

### Hand 5: Architect (2 Triples + 1 Ladder)
- **Focus:** Balance triple accumulation with ladder building
- **Risk Level:** High
- **Tip:** Get your ladder started early

### Hand 6: Duel (1 Triple + 2 Ladders)
- **Focus:** Strong ladder suit commitment
- **Risk Level:** High
- **Tip:** Your triple should come naturally

### Hand 7: Master (3 Ladders)
- **Focus:** Three coherent ladder paths
- **Risk Level:** Very High
- **Tip:** Fewer buysâ€”use them wisely
- **Warning:** This hand decides many championships

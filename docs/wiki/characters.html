Characters: The Four Minds

Habib Punja is designed to feel like a strategy night with friends‚Äîexcept some of those "friends" are AI minds with distinct, learnable personalities.

Habot and Jabot are AI players.
MuBot and Joko are commentators/advisors (not players).

Habot (Rabbit) üê∞ ‚Äî The Aggressive Tactician

  • Role: AI opponent (player)
  • Personality: Fast, bold, impulse-forward, aggressive, opportunistic.
  • Style: Early publishing, momentum plays, controlled chaos

Strengths

  • Wins tempo and forces reactions
  • Punishes hesitation
  • Strong early-game pressure
  • Capitalizes on opponent mistakes

Weaknesses

  • Higher risk of nukes (devastating losses)
  • Can overbuy or publish prematurely
  • Sometimes sacrifices stability for speed
  • Vulnerable to patient counter-play

Player Counter-Strategy

"Play the war, not the hand."
Bait Habot into overcommitting. Let the rabbit run too fast and stumble.

Jabot (Tortoise) üê¢ ‚Äî The Patient Strategist

  • Role: AI opponent (player)
  • Personality: Calm, deliberate, threshold-based, patient, defensive, precise.
  • Style: Waits for structure, publishes when stable

Strengths

  • Disciplined buying (rarely wastes buys)
  • Minimizes waste and risk
  • Strong late-hand execution
  • Consistent across all 7 hands

Weaknesses

  • May give tempo away to aggressive players
  • Can stall if they don't get perfect cards
  • Predictable playstyle

Player Counter-Strategy

"Speed kills structure."
Push the pace. If you publish early, Jabot may panic or be caught holding too many high-value cards.

MuBot (Zen Master) üßò ‚Äî The Advisor

Role: Commentator / Guide

MuBot sits near the Draw Deck, representing the "New Potential." He focuses on long-term strategy, patience, and the flow of the game.

When MuBot Speaks:

  • When you make a wise long-term move
  • When you avoid a dangerous buy
  • When the game state requires patience
  • To calm you after a bad beat

Joko (Provocateur) üé≠ ‚Äî The Hype Man

Role: Commentator / Hype Engine

Joko sits near the Discard Pile, representing "Chaos and Action." He loves risk, big plays, and dramatic moments.

When Joko Speaks:

  • Game start (building excitement)
  • Big plays (buys, publications)
  • Close hands (tension building)
  • Championship finale (celebration)

For Players & Testers

If you’re play-testing Habib Punja (Public Beta) and something feels off, we’d love to hear from you. Clear reports help us make the AI smarter and the experience smoother—for everyone.

When you reach out, it helps if you can include:

  • Your device & browser (e.g., “iPhone 16 / Safari”, “Windows 11 / Chrome”).
  • Which hand / phase you were in (e.g., Hand 3, building ladders, after two buys).
  • What you expected to happen vs. what actually happened.
  • Any screenshots or short descriptions of AI behavior that felt odd, brilliant, or broken.
MuBot’s note: Every small detail you share helps refine the system. Even “it just felt wrong here” is valuable when we match it with the game logs and AI decisions.
